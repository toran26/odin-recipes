Rice Pudding Recipe

In Nepal,Rice Pudding - Khir is a delicious staple dessert. With the addition of spices like cloves, fenugreek, cardamom, and cinnamon, it resembles European rice pudding. After a filling curry, this tasty pudding will sate your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Dryfruits
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Heat the pot and add some ghee
  2. Fry the rice until it gets brown
  3. Add Milk and dry fruits and cook it
  4. Cook it until milk condenses and your rice looks like thick liquid
  5. Enjoy!
