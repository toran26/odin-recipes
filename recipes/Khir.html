Rice Pudding Recipe

Rice Puddingis a popular steamed from Tibetan and Nepali cuisine, filled with minced meat (chicken, buffalo, pork) or vegetables (cabbage, onion, tofu) inside a thin dough wrapper, served with spicy dipping sauce, and is a beloved street food and staple in the Himalayas.

Ingredients

Wrappers

  • All purpose flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Fillings

  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Onions
  • Chicken meat
  • Ginger
  • Momo Masala
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough. Knead it properly using lukewarm water and let it rest, covered with a damp cloth.
  2. Prepare chicken filling by turnig all the filling ingredients into finely minced versions.
  3. Roll the wrappers using the dough and fill the momo with chicken filling and give shape.
  4. Steam the momo. Dont forget to apply oil to the tray of you steamer to avoid momo from sticking
